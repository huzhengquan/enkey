# Lesson 1

Every day, / 每一天
I get up, / 我起床
I eat breakfast, / 我吃早饭
I go to school, / 我去上学
I eat lunch, / 我吃午饭
I go home, / 我回家
I eat dinner, / 我吃晚饭
I go to bed, / 我睡觉
Every day!  /每一天
